About Us

Founded by Ayah and Amr Ebada, we’re dedicated to showcasing the vibrant essence of Ottawa and beyond through our diverse array of content. At Yoyo’s DSB, we’re not just content creators; we’re storytellers on a mission to captivate and inspire. Through our blogs, films, photography, and projects, we offer a fresh perspective that celebrates the richness and dynamism of our world. In addition to our captivating content, we provide exciting opportunities for photography and videography enthusiasts as well as services for local businesses in Ottawa. Whether you’re looking to hone your skills or elevate your brand, we’re here to support and inspire you every step of the way.

Ayah Ebada: Co-Owner

Also known as Yoyo to close family & friends; Ayah created her DSB originally as a way to express herself. However with her rising success she has established herself as a creative & business woman. Ayah is experienced with videography & photography and is always coming up with new project ideas. Her vibrant personality emitting from her work is what makes her stand out from the rest.

Amr Ebada: Co-Owner

Amr is an aspiring business man who handles all of the finances & business meetings with clients. He is the brain of the operation when his co-owner Ayah is on her creative ventures. At the age of 4 he has attained his business degree from Harvard and plans on expanding Yoyo's DSB to its full potential. He is very proud of his sister and hopes to be in Forbes 30 under 30 by next year.
